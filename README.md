## 🎓 Academic Profile & Research Interests
*I am an active researcher and software engineer seeking a PhD position at the **Technical University of Munich (TUM)**. My core research bridges the gap between environmental perception, explainable machine learning (XAI), and robust system engineering. I am deeply passionate about developing **Trustworthy AI** systems, focusing on Multimodal Fusion, Computer Vision, and addressing real-world socio-technical challenges.*

### 🔬 Research Focus Areas
* **Explainable & Trustworthy AI:** Formalizing justification mechanisms for hallucinations in Generative AI; developing robust evaluation frameworks for enhanced predictive reliability.
* **Computer Vision & Automated Systems:** AI-driven proctoring systems, real-time activity detection, and multi-scale local binary pattern-convolutional neural networks (MSLBP-CNN) for medical diagnostics.
* **Algorithmic Bias & Inclusivity:** Quantifying algorithmic bias, addressing the "White Default" paradigm, and integrating African narratives into AI models.
* **Distributed Computing & Optimization:** Task scheduling in Fog-Cloud computing utilizing Machine Learning and Metaheuristic approaches.
